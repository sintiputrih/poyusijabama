Who is Marisa Ramirez?

Marisa Ramirez is an American actress who started her acting career portraying the role of Liza in the TV series USA High’.

It was sure that she would try her luck in show business following her photo shoot when she was 13. The charismatic actress states that she is of Mexican, Irish, and American Indian ethnicity.

Sexy Marisa is popularly known for her role as NYPD detective Maria Beaz in the popular TV series ‘Blue Bloods’.

What are the birth facts of Marisa Ramirez?

Marisa was born on 15 September 1977, in Los Angeles, California, United States.

Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is mixed(Mexican, Irish, and American Indian).

She was raised in the neighborhood of California. Her birth name is Marisa Maguire Ramirez. There is no information on her parents and siblings.

Marisa Ramirez grew up with an interest in posing for a camera which would land her a successful modeling and acting career in the future.

Education

Marisa Ramirez graduated from All Saints Catholic School. For her further education, she joined an all-girl school (Ramona Convent Secondary School) which is located in Alhambra, California. She graduated from there in 1995.

Marisa Ramirez: Professional Life and Career

Marisa Ramirez got the international modeling break when the Polaroid photo of then 13 years Marisa was discovered by a famous LA modeling agency.

This gave her international fame as a model. She got to travel to different parts of the world for her photo shoots with different companies.

After her successful career in modeling, she also tried her luck in hosting and she did a pretty good job with it.

Her fame earned by successful modeling landed her first acting role in the TV series USA High’.

It was in 2003 when she played her first major role in the TV series ‘Miracles’ as Evelyn Santos. She was also featured in the TV film ‘Hitched’.

As of now, Marisa Ramirez is famously known for her role as NYPD detective Maria Beaz in the popular TV series ‘Blue Bloods’.
