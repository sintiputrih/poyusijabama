Earning From Business

Phil founded the Duck Commander Company in 1973. Willie Robertson, his son, is the company’s CEO.

The enterprise has developed into a multi-million dollar duck hunting operation in Louisiana, United States.

Phil’s company has grown so large that he now employs his entire family, including his wife, brother, and four kids, to help him operate it.

FAQs

How was the childhood of Phil Robertson?

During his childhood, Phil witnessed his family struggling financially. They lived in a dingy treehouse with no running water, power, or bathrooms. The family made a living by growing fruits and vegetables in their garden and hunting rabbits, trout, and squirrels in the woods.

Why did Robertson decide not to play football?

Phil Robertson was a football enthusiast and was quite good at it. But he quit football because it interfered with his greatest passion: hunting season.

Why does Phil Robertson have beards?

Robertson and other members of the Duck Dynasty are usually seen with long beards. When it gets fairly cold, it’s an excellent method to cover your face when you’re out in the duck blind for hours on end.
