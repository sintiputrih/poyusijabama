Meet YouTuber Jake Koehler

Jake Koehler (age 31, December 18, 1991) is an American YouTuber who runs a YouTube channel named “DALLMYD.” The young internet celebrity uploads videos regarding his underwater treasure hunting.

Currently, the YT star serves over 13.5 million subscribers, but this count will soon skyrocket because of what he recently uploaded on his channel.

On June 24, Koehler shared a video on YouTube where he revealed that a few weeks before the OceanGate submarine accident, he was on the exact same submarine. Reportedly he visited Stockton Rush, founder of OceanGate, and experienced what it’s like being in a deep diving submarine.

The YouTuber even shared glimpses of his visit where he not only showed the inside of Titan, the submersible, but also showed what goes into preparing a ride to the Titanic.

Jake Koehler’s Time Inside Titan 

In an astonishing video shared on his YouTube channel, Jake Koehler took his audience on a virtual journey inside the Titan submersible. Prior to the sub’s mysterious disappearance, Koehler documented his nine-day excursion in the Atlantic Ocean, offering a unique perspective into the world of deep-sea exploration.

The footage showcased OceanGate CEO Stockton Rush expressing concerns about the sub’s control systems, providing a rare behind-the-scenes glimpse into the thrilling and sometimes perilous realm of underwater expeditions.

Koehler, with his characteristic enthusiasm and authenticity, shared nearly 30 minutes of captivating footage with his viewers. From his journey to St. John’s in Newfoundland, Canada, to his rendezvous with the Titan submersible, every frame brimmed with intrigue.

The video encapsulated the excitement, challenges, and ultimate rewards of venturing into uncharted waters, captivating viewers and reinforcing the allure of Koehler’s channel. Tragically, the Titan submersible met a grim fate during its fifth mission on June 18, leaving behind a trail of unanswered questions.

Koehler’s video shed light on Stockton Rush’s concerns about the sub’s control systems, adding a sobering touch to the thrilling adventure. Subsequent investigations by the US Navy revealed that the sub imploded shortly after its descent, leaving the ocean depths to guard its secrets.

How Old is Jake Koehler?

On December 18, 1991, Jake Koehler (age 31)was born to his mother, Lori, and father, Timothy Koehler, in Georgia, United States. He has a sister named Hannah and two half-brothers: Andrew and Thomas Gotham.

Inspired by his passion for the underwater world, Jake set out to turn it into a career opportunity that would allow him to share his discoveries with the world.

Jake Koehler’s Career 

Jake Koehler’s journey as a YouTuber began on February 1, 2011, when he created his channel, “DALLMYD.” In the early days, his content primarily featured Call of Duty – Black Ops gameplay videos, which garnered positive responses from viewers.

Recognizing the potential of sharing his passions beyond gaming, Jake expanded his content to include surfing videos, showcasing his versatility as a content creator.

It was a pivotal moment when Jake uploaded a video featuring an underwater treasure hunt. The video struck a chord with his audience, capturing their imagination and resonating deeply.

Encouraged by the overwhelming response, Jake focused his efforts on creating captivating underwater treasure-hunting videos. The combination of adventure, discovery, and the thrill of uncovering lost artifacts proved to be a winning formula, propelling his channel to new heights.

Today, “DALLMYD” boasts an impressive subscriber count of over 13.5 million, a testament to Jake Koehler’s ability to engage and captivate his audience.

Beyond the joy of sharing his experiences, his channel has become a significant source of income. Revenue from his Google AdSense account, coupled with brand promotions, ensures that Jake’s passion for exploration is both fulfilling and sustainable.
