Who are Dwight Howard's Kids?

 Posted on October 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  519 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Dwight Howard has been in the NBA since 2004. He is best known for his days with the Orlando Magic where he made multiple All-NBA, NBA All-Star, and NBA All-Defensive teams. In 2009, he also led them to the finals. During 2007-2012, he was considered the best center and defender in the league, picking up the Defensive Player of the Year Award three years on the bounce.

Although the second half of his career did not turn out as expected, Dwight had a renaissance period during his later spells with the Lakers. As a role player, he won an NBA Championship during the 2019-2020 NBA Season. The character he has shown during the twilight period of his career, his humility, team-first attitude, and his readiness to perform has translated to his fatherhood.

How many kids does Dwight Howard have?

Earlier in his life, Dwight Howards' reckless decisions led to him having five children with five different women. But he has made sure he is present as a father to all five of his kids and a co-parent to all five of his children’s mothers. In a video alongside Graham Bensinger, he talks about how life is raising five children to the best possible capacity.

The names of his children are Braylon, Jayde, Layla, David, and Dwight III or Trey. He is alleged to have eight kids, but has claimed to have only five. The names of his baby-mamas are: Royce Reed, Christine Vest, Hope Alexa, Tiffany Render, and Melissa Rios.

Who are Dwight Howard's Kids?

Braylon Howard

Born on November 18, 2007, Braylon Howard is Dwight’s first child. Braylon’s mother is Royce Reed, a former Orlando Magic cheerleader and a reality show actor.

Layla Howard

Born in October 2010, Layla Howard is Dwight’s second child. Layla’s mother is Tiffany Render.
