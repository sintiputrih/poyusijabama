Michael Dakessian Job

Besides his TV show gig, Michael Dakessian is a mixed martial art fighter and an internet personality. He holds a black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. He honed his MMA skills at Pride Brazilian Jiu Jitsu.

He has been awarded numerous competitions over the years. As of the latest amateur MMA record, he has 4 wins, 2 losses, and 0 draws.

Michael participated in his last fight on 3 July 2021 in CFFC. The last time he checked, Mike weighed 166.5 lbs thus he played in the welterweight (weight class). According to Topology, he has been ranked 38th of 255 active US Northeast Amateur Welterweights, and 16th of 77 active Pennsylvania Amateur Welterweights, and 4th out of 16 active New Jersey Amateur Welterweights.

His MMA fights have also landed him the opportunity to represent brands such as Brotrition and Eat Clean Bro. He also sells merch (hoodies, caps, t-shirts) on his website with his logo.
