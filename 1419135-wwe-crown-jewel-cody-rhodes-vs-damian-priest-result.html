WWE Crown Jewel: Cody Rhodes vs. Damian Priest Result

 Posted on October 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  335 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Cody Rhodes got some revenge on The Judgment Day this afternoon by defeating Damian Preist at WWE Crown Jewel.

The finish saw The American Nightmare hit three Cross Rhodes on Damian Priest to secure the pinfall victory.

WWE Crown Jewel Results

Check out a recap of the match, courtesy of our live coverage page:

Before the bell rings, Priest attacks Rhodes. The bell rings, and Rhodes lands a flurry of strikes. Rhodes lands a side effect. Priest rolls out of the ring. Rhodes tries a dive, but Priest walks out of the way. Priest lands a bunch of kicks. Rhodes leapfrogs over Priest, but tweaks his ankle in the process. Priest floors Rhodes with a tornado right hand. Priest lands a stage dive. Rhodes catches Priest with a dropkick.

Cody tweaks his ankle on another leapfrog but is able to land the disaster kick. Rhodes clears off the commentary desk. Priest turns Rhodes inside out with a lariat. Priest tries to hit a Razor’s Edge through the table, but Rhodes escapes. Dive by Rhodes. Rhodes sets up Crossroads on the commentary desk. Priest reverses it into the Reckoning. Priest sends Rhodes back into the ring for another Reckoning. Rhodes reverses that into Crossroads. Finn Bálor runs down to the ring.

The Finish

In the confusion, JD McDonagh tries a sneak attack, but Rhodes turns him back. Priest chokeslams Rhodes. Rhodes kicks out. Bálor yells for Dominik. Dominik walks to the ring with a chair. Jey Uso hopes the barricade and superkicks Dominik. Jey proceeds to superkicks Bálor and McDonagh. Rhodes lands a Cody Cutter back in the ring. Bionic elbow by Rhodes. Rhodes hits a mega Cody Cutter off the top. Priest somehow kicks out. After a series of reversals, Rhodes hits three consecutive Crossroads for the win.

Winner- Cody Rhodes
