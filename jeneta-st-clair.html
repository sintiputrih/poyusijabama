Jeneta St. Clair is an American Actress, writer, producer, a comedian from Sheboygan, Wisconsin (a city in the United States of America) known for her work in Ernesto Wood (2010), After Midnight (2014), The Appearing (2014), Gut-to-Go (2010).

Jeneta has raised in Indiana till her high school; after that, she went to American Musical and Dramatic Academy in Los Angeles. When she completed her study, she dived into the film industry.

Jeneta St. Clair Career 

Jeneta started her career in 2011 with the short comedy romance Gut-to-Go which was rated 9 out of 10 by IMDB. Giving continuity to her film career, she completed 16 TV Episodes, 14 Feature Films, 8 TV Series, 6 Short Films,3 TV Movies, and three videos throughout the years.

Some of her career credits include The Newest Pledge, I Didn’t Know I Was Pregnant, Smith & Wesson, After Midnight, The Middle, Mudd Man, Valley Drive, and many others.
