Elizabeth Alvarez Biography

Elizabeth Alvarez is an American award-winning, bilingual Journalist, who was born and raised in Southern California. Currently, she works at KUSI-TV in San Diego, California as a Morning News Anchor and Reporter. Prior to joining the KUSI news team, she worked at LIN Media / KRQE-TV Channel 13 in Albuquerque, New Mexico, as a News Anchor.

Elizabeth worked at Fox 35 Orlando, WOFL-TV in Orlando, Florida as an Anchor and Reporter, and at KFOX-TV in El Paso, Texas as a Morning News Anchor. She served as a Reporter at KDBC-TV, and as a News Assistant at KCBS. She also as a One-Man-Band for KOBR-TV.
