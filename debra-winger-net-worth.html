Debra Winger is an American actress. Her Hollywood career began in the 1970s. She became more popular in the ’80s, first with the movie Urban Cowboy, for which she received Golden Globe and BAFTA nominations. She later starred in the movie An Officer and a Gentleman, which got her an Academy Award nomination for Best Actress. She was again acclaimed for her performance in the film Terms of Endearment. In the ’90s, she was best known for starring works in Shadowlands, A Dangerous Woman, and Leap of Faith. In the 2000s, her most notable work was in the drama film Rachel Getting Married. In the 2010s, she starred as Frances in the series In Treatment. As of 2016, she plays Maggie Bennett in the Netflix series The Ranch.
