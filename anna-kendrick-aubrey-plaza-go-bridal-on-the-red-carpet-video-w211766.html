Anna Kendrick, Aubrey Plaza Go Bridal on the Red Carpet: Video

 Posted on September 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  259 words  |  Aldo Pusey

We’d hang out with this group at any wedding! The cast of Mike and Dave Need Wedding Dates — Anna Kendrick, Aubrey Plaza, Zac Efron and Adam DeVine — turned it out for the film’s Hollywood premiere. See their styles in Red Carpet Daily, presented by AT&T!

Embracing the bridal theme of the movie, Kendrick, 30, donned a bustier-style Calvin Klein cocktail dress, teamed with black Jimmy Choo ankle-strap sandals. The Into the Woods actress accessorized with a Jimmy Choo bag, plus jewels by Le Vian, Maxior and Anita Ko.

PHOTOS: Zac Efron’s Buff Body

Plaza clearly got the white memo, too! The Parks and Recreation alum, 32, rocked a sequined Prada sheath dress, accented by two blue panels. She also carried a Jimmy Choo clutch and added metallic Casadei sandals.

While the boys did not wear white, they certainly didn’t disappoint. Ever the dapper gent, Efron, 28, chose a taupe suit, while DeVine, 32, opted for blue plaid — a choice, he joked, that meant to compete with his handsome costar. Watch the video to see them hamming it up together on the red carpet!
