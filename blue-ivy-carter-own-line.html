Blue Ivy Is Rumored to Be Launching Her Own Line

 Posted on February 14, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  186 words  |  Aldo Pusey

We're still recovering from the news that Beyoncé is expecting twins, but the Carters might have some more news coming down the pipe: Blue Ivy is reportedly getting her own line. (She's only five years old, but when you have a superstar mom and raper-turned-mogul dad, what can't you do?)

Beyoncé's company is rumored to be creating a line of Blue Ivy Carter products, which could include anything from hair-care, clothing, video games and more, according to TMZ. The legal docs are already filed and "the application will be approved very soon," the site reports. Apparently, the singer tried doing the same in 2012 but was rejected because "Blue Ivy" was already trademarked.

Meanwhile, another celebrity toddler is also starting out her business career, this time in the design realm. Four-year-old North West is currently playing co-designer for her parent's children's line.

Kids these days.
