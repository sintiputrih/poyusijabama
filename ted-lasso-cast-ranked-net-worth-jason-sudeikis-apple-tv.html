The Cast Of 'Ted Lasso' Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on June 18, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  989 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The sensation of Ted Lasso has got fans wondering... how much do these talented actors and actresses actually make? Jason Sudeikis is the lead protagonist of the series, and he has been in many iconic films. The cast also consists of Hannah Waddingham, Juno Temple, Brett Goldstein, Brendan Hunt, Phil Dunster, Nick Mohammed, Toheeb Jimoh, Jeremy Swift, and many more. The show was renewed for a third season and the cast and writers are set to receive a large pay raise.

Ted Lasso dominated at the Emmys, winning four awards including Outstanding Comedy Series and Outstanding Actor in a Comedy Series. Apple TV+ broke records for being the first streaming service to land an Emmy Award in a program category in only its second year of eligibility. The show and the cast are so beloved, so their salaries seem well deserved. Let's see how much the cast of Ted Lasso is currently worth!

9 Toheeb Jimoh: $350,000

Toheeb Jimoh is an English actor known for his work in the series Ted Lasso, London Kills, and the Feed. Although he has only been acting for a couple of years, his role as Sam Obisanya in the series quickly made him a fan favorite. His secret romance on the series showed his softer side and made his character even more adorable.

8 Juno Temple: $1 Million

Juno Temple is best known for her roles in the films Wild Child, Atonement, St Trinian's, Little Birds, Killer Joe, The Dark Knight Rises, and Afternoon Delight. She won over the world with her portrayal of the sweet Keeley Jones in Ted Lasso. Her romance with bad boy Roy Kent has become one of the most precious onscreen relationships. Their cheeky flirts and witty banter are what makes this couple so endearing.

“The feedback that I’ve had from people that have watched the show and have enjoyed the show, talk about the joy that has brought them during this time,” she told Access Hollywood. “I don’t know if this will ever happen again in my career, but I felt the same way making it.

7 Phil Dunster: $1 to $5 Million

Phil Dunster plays the epic football player Jamie Tartt, whose ego isolated him from the team. He's made a name for himself as an actor in the U.K. Dunster also starred in the productions The Trouble with Maggie Cole, Humans, Save Me, and Strike Back. Phil Dunster is also a recurring guest in Hulu's Catherine the Great.

6 Nick Mohammed: $1.5 Million

Nick is a British actor, comedian, and writer who has appeared in numerous sitcoms including Ted Lasso. He has also been in the films Christopher Robin, The Martian, and Bridget Jones's Baby and television shows like Intelligence, Drifters, and Sorry, I've Got No Head. Mohammed scored his first Emmy nomination for his character Nate, who seems to have turned to the dark side in season two.

5 Brendan Hunt: $3 Million

Brendan Hunt is best known for his roles in We're The Millers and Horrible Bosses 2 which his co-star Jason Sudeikis starred in. He is a co-creator and star of the Apple TV+ sitcom. Coach Beard is a man of few words, but he is Ted's right-hand man and so the fans trust him.

4 Jeremy Swift: $4 Million

Jeremy's career in the acting business has lasted over three decades and is still kicking. Swift plays the deliciously awkward Leslie Higgins who is the club's communications director. Jeremy Swift also appeared in the popular series Downton Abbey and the critically acclaimed film Mary Poppins Returns.

3 Brett Goldstein: $5 to $10 Million

Brett acts in this sitcom and also helped create it, and he won a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series. No one could have played Roy Kent other than Brett Goldstein. He delivered one of the most iconic lines from the show to Rebecca, when he stated: "You deserve someone who makes you feel like you’ve been struck by f***ing lightning. Don’t you dare settle for fine."

He wrote and appeared in the film SuperBob and wrote the Catherine Tate Live Show, and has also performed multiple solo stand-up shows. He was initially just a writer on Ted Lasso and while they were writing the part of Roy, he knew that he could execute it and he was right!

2 Hannah Waddingham: $5.5 Million

With Waddingham's growing popularity for her character on Ted Lasso, her bank account has followed suit. Hannah portrays the vindictive Rebecca Welton who wanted to destroy her ex-husband's football club... and Ted with it. Her character ends up falling for Ted's charm and unbelievably pure ways and they end up being the best of friends.

Hannah has an unbelievable singing voice and was actually in musicals before joining television. In 2015, she landed the role of Septa Unella in the HBO drama Game of Thrones. She also starred in Netflix's Sex Education, but Ted Lasso is what put her on the map!

1 Jason Sudeikis: $25 Million

In 2003, Sudeikis got his big break on Saturday Night Live as a writer and in 2005 he became a featured performer on the show. He broke into the film industry a few years later with roles in Watching the Detectives, The Ten, and Meet Bill. Since then, Sudeikis has been in a number of popular films like We're the Millers, Horrible Bosses, and Hall Pass.

Sudeikis will receive a lengthy pay raise with a whopping $1 million per episode for his starring role in Ted Lasso. He was initially making about $250,000 to $300,000 per episode so this will definitely increase the actor's current net worth.
