The Bold and the Beautiful Week of May 13 Spoilers: Sheila Shocker Hits FC Finns Backlash

 Posted on April 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  424 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers for the week of May 13-17 reveal that Sheila Carter’s (Kimberlin Brown) survival will make more waves in Los Angeles. It won’t be long until the Sheila shocker strikes Forrester Creations, so Brooke Logan (Katherine Kelly Lang) and Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) will learn the truth.

Of course, that means Ridge and Brooke will find out “Sugar” aka Janet Webber (Kimberlin Brown) is the person who perished at the cliff house that night – not the real Sheila like everyone previously thought.

So many people called Deacon delusional when he claimed he saw 10 toes in the crematorium, but Deacon knew what he was talking about.

Brooke, Ridge and plenty of other naysayers will have to accept that Sheila is truly alive.

Naturally, that won’t be good news as far as Ridge is concerned! Ridge may wish that Deacon and John “Finn” Finnegan (Tanner Novlan) had never found Sheila in that old warehouse.

You can bet Finn will face backlash for his newfound support of Sheila. Ridge, Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood), Li Finnegan (Naomi Matsuda), Liam Spencer (Scott Clifton) and others around LA will slam Finn for letting Sheila pull the wool over his eyes.

Hope Logan (Annika Noelle) will remain the only person Finn feels he can turn to without judgment, so it’ll be interesting to watch their bond grow from here.

Sheila may see the potential for a new love interest for Finn depending on how close he gets to Hope in the coming B&B episodes!

As for Steffy, she’ll be frustrated over Finn’s willingness to overlook Sheila’s past mistakes.

Steffy may seek Liam’s comfort as she deals with sudden marriage turmoil since she never expected Finn’s birth mom to become an issue again.

Naturally, Li will be hurt and furious over Finn accepting Sheila into his life after everything she’s done to cause pain.

Although Li will blast Finn’s decision to extend love and acceptance to Sheila, he may stand his ground.

Next, Deacon Sharpe (Sean Kanan) will hope to give Sheila the gift of an amazing wedding, so it sounds like he’ll get her input and start planning a special day to remember!

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers for the week of May 13-17 will bring some heated confrontations and escalating drama, so we’ll bring you more story predictions and updates on all the mayhem.
