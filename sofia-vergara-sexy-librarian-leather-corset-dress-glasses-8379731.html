Sofia Vergara Gave Sexy Librarian By Pairing a Leather Corset Dress With the Most Practical Accessor

 Posted on September 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  308 words  |  Martina Birk

As one of Hollywood’s most notable bombshells, Sofia Vergara definitely knows how to bring the spice to any outfit she wears (see: sheer lace corsets and matching rhinestone-covered sets). But while see-through tops and glitzy accessories may be easy to dress up on their own, the actress just proved that even glasses can be incredibly sexy when styled correctly.

On Monday, the A-lister shared the sartorial lesson with her 31.6 million Instagram followers by posting a pair of photos to her account. In the snaps, Vergara posed in a black leather corset dress that featured a strapless neckline, plunging bust, and a midi length. Fully committing to the sexy librarian look, the actress added a pair of Foster Grant brown-rimmed glasses to match the studious setting she was surrounded by, and she finished her ‘fit by accessorizing with a simple silver bracelet.

As for glam, Sofia wore her brunette hair down in beachy waves with a middle part (sorry, no pencils in messy buns here) and rounded out the look by adding a bronzy contour, feathered lashes, and a light brown lip.

“Buy a reader, give a reader!” she captioned the post, which was a promo for her ongoing partnership with Foster Grant. “For every pair you purchase, a pair of reading glasses is donated to people in underserved communities around the world. #SeeBeautifully❤️”

Vergara’s post comes just days after it was revealed that she’s feeling as good as she looks in the wake of her divorce from Joe Manganiello, which was first revealed back in July. According to a source for People, the America’s Got Talent judge is reportedly “dating and having fun.”

“She is enjoying life. She seems positive and full of energy," the source shared. “Sofia has been handling the divorce well. It wasn’t an easy decision, but she is doing great now.”
