Career and Professional Life

Punit Pathak is a famous Indian dancer, social media personality, and actor. He first made his appearance in the dance reality show Dance India Dance season 2. Furthermore, In the year 2015, the dancer starred in the dance film, Disney’s ABCD 2, which was released on 19 June 2015. Likewise, as his popularity grew he became the mentor on the dance reality show Dance Plus (season 2), and Dance Plus (season 3). The Indian dancer was also a choreographer in Dance Champions.

Furthermore, In the year 2018, the dancer starred in the romantic comedy film Nawabzaade.
