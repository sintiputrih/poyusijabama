Auston Matthews Wiki

Auston Taylour Matthews, better known by his stage as Auston Matthews, was born on September 17, 1997, in San Ramon, California, USA. He is currently 26 years old and is a citizen of the United States. He also bears the Virgo zodiac sign and is of Mexican-American descent. In terms of his parentage, he was born to Brian Matthews (father) and Ema Matthews (mother) (mother). His father was a collegiate baseball player, and his mother is Mexican. Alexandria, his older sister, and Breyana, his younger sister, are his siblings. His parents relocated to Scottsdale, Arizona when he was a child.

As a kid, he and his parents used to go to the local ‘Phoenix Coyotes’ games. As a result, he developed a passion for ice hockey. When he was five years old, he began to demonstrate an interest in the game. He used to play baseball and ice hockey as a kid, and he was particularly good at baseball.

Professional Life

The ‘Everett Silvertips’ drafted Auston in the ‘WHL Bantam Draft’ in 2012, however he chose to play for the “US National U17” squad instead. His performance astounded ice hockey aficionados. The following year, he scored 55 goals, 61 assists, and 116 points for the “US National U18” team. He finished first, shattering Patrick Kane’s 102-point ‘National Team Development Program’ record set between 2005 and 2006.

During his second game with the ‘U-17 NTDP,’ he broke his femur in a knee-to-knee collision in September 2013. He needed surgery, but he was back in December 2013, with 12 goals and 33 points in 24 games. Later in the season, he also played for the ‘U-18 NTDP.’
