Isla Fisher and Sacha Baron Cohen were always one of Hollywood's most private pairs. While Fisher sometimes talked about her husband's comedy, one of the most important facts about the couple is that they didn't talk about each other. But even though Fisher and Baron Cohen were always mysterious, they looked happy in photos and there was never any sign that anything was wrong. That's why when Page Six reported in early April 2024 that the couple decided to end their marriageafter 14 years, it was definitely shocking.

In this article, we'll take a look at what we know about Sacha Baron Cohen and Isla Fisher's marriage before their divorce announcement. We'll also look at whether Rebel Wilson's allegations against the actor had anything to do with the split.

What Was Sacha Baron Cohen And Isla Fisher's Marriage Like Before Their Divorce?

While fans know that Isla Fisher helped her husband get around an Oscar rule when he was a presenter, Fisher and Sacha Baron Cohen's marriage has always been somewhat of a secret. However, Isla Fisher explained the reason that she kept their relationship private.

In an interview with People in 2022, she said:

"I get nervous talking about it because I feel like, by not having my relationship in the public domain and not having spoken about how we met or really talked about our marriage publicly, it's remained something private and valuable to me."

Her next comments suggested that the marriage was happy and that they bonded over their sense of humor. She said:

"I don't know if that's the secret. But having a shared ability to find humor in the sharpness of life is always going to bring connection. And, otherwise, I just think it's nice to keep some things for yourself."

While some celebrities keep their weddings private, others go a step further and almost never talk about their partner. Even though Fisher has worked steadily throughout her career and is interviewed often, she rarely said much about her bond with Baron Cohen.

While the couple seemed to joke around and make each other laugh all the time, not many details are known about their marriage. However, according to The Daily Mail, fans should know a few things about their relationship.

Sacha Baron Cohen and Isla Fisher seemed to live a regular life with their kids. They began living in Sydney in 2020 and then Perth, and it sounds like that was a better fit for them than Los Angeles. Fisher said that she liked staying home and having people over because they have three kids. She didn't find LA to be as family-friendly.

Fisher talked about the pair's personal life in a 2016 interview on The Late Late Show with James Corden and said that her husband sometimes wore the same clothing and mustache as Borat when they were intimate.

Anytime that Fisher spoke about her marriage, she painted a fairly normal picture, and no one would even know that they were famous based on her comments. She also never alluded to any problems, and he never did, either.

Did Rebel Wilson's Allegations Against Sacha Baron Cohen Lead To His Divorce From Isla Fisher?

Just like people were curious about what Sacha Baron Cohen's relationship with Rebel Wilson was like before her allegations, fans naturally wondered if these allegations were why Baron Cohen and Isla Fisher announced their divorce.

According to Entertainment Tonight, this isn't the case at all. The couple wrote a statement together that they both shared on their social media accounts and explained that they broke up in 2023. Wilson talked about Baron Cohen in 2024.
