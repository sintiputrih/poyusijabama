When Did John Madden Die?

John Madden died on December 28, 2021, at the age of 85.

NFL announced his death with this message ' On behalf of the entire NFL family, we extend our condolences to Virginia, Mike, Joe, and their families," said NFL commissioner Roger Goodell.

Commissioner also added, " Nobody loved football more than Coach. He was football. There will never be another Jhon Madden. We will be indebted to him to make football and the NFL the way it is today."

John Madden Disease And Health

John Madden underwent open-heart surgery in 2015

He has been hospitalized several times for health issues related to his heart, knee, hip, and recently for his esophagus.

Madden also retired from football because of his health issues and old age.

Who Is John Madden Wife: His Family Revealed

John Madden is married to his wife, Virginia Feilds.

They have been a partner for more than six decades. The couple met at a bar in Pismo Beach, California, and tied their knot on December 26, 1959.

Madden has two sons, Joseph, who played for Brown University, and Michael, who played for Harvard University. They both are settled in Pleasanton, California
