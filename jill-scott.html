Jill Scott Relationship Status

  • Scott and long-time friend Lyzel Williams, a graphic artist and DJ, were married during a private Hawaiian holiday ceremony in 2001. The couple had been dating for seven years before they got married. Scott wrote and recorded a song about Williams, “He Loves Me (Lyzel in E Flat). Following six years of marriage, Scott and Williams were divorced in 2007.
  • On June 20, 2008, Scott shared a long on-stage kiss with her drummer, Li'l John Roberts, at a concert in New York's Carnegie Hall. The pair then told the crowd that they were engaged. Their son, Jett Hamilton Roberts, was born on April 20th, 2009.
  • On June 23, 2009, Scott revealed that she and Roberts had broken up, with Scott breaking the news for Essence Magazine. Given the break-up, Scott hopes that both parents will take an active role in their child's life, saying, “We really love our son and we're co-parenting and working on becoming friends. That's all it is. I have a lot of support, and I don't want anything to do with that, Scott referred to the break-up as some of her new music inspiration.
  • On June 25, 2016, Scott married another long-term partner, Mike Dobson, at a private ceremony.
  • In 2017, after 15 months of marriage, she filed a divorce with Dobson.
