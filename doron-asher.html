All About Israeli mother of Two Doron Asher 

Doron Asher (age 34, born February 24, 1989) is an Israeli mother who, along with her two daughters, was tragically captured by Hamas terrorists on October 7. This horrifying event occurred when Hamas militants infiltrated the area and took over 100 civilians, including Doron and her daughters, hostage.

On that fateful day, Doron had taken her young girls to visit their grandmother in the village of Nir Oz near the Gaza border. Tragically, their peaceful visit was disrupted when Hamas militants descended upon the home.

Yoni Asher, Doron’s husband, shared that his wife managed to make a distressing call before the line was abruptly cut. In that call, she informed him about the presence of terrorists inside their home. Yoni suspects that Efrat Katz, his mother-in-law who was also with Doron and the children, might have been kidnapped by the Hamas terrorists.

In this harrowing time, Mr. Asher is fervently praying for the safe return of his beloved family. He pleads with Hamas to spare his family and to consider him as a captive in their stead, urging for their release.

Doron is Missing With Her Daughters and Mother

On October 7, 2023, Doron Asher found herself at her mother’s home in Nir Oz, a peaceful village near Gaza, completely unaware of the impending danger. Her daughters, Raz and Aviv, were beside her and her mother, Efrat Katz. In this sanctuary, the tranquility was shattered as Hamas terrorists breached the border, launching an attack on the border cities and villages of Israel.

This ruthless Islamic terror group not only targeted police stations, military outposts, and security forces but also unleashed their aggression upon innocent civilians. Shockingly, many civilians were injured, some fatally, and many were captured and forcibly taken to Gaza as hostages.

When Doron sensed the approaching danger, she acted swiftly, reaching out to her husband, Yoni Asher, desperately alerting him to the imminent threat at her home. She conveyed the terrifying news that armed terrorists were closing in on their residence. Their conversation was tragically brief before the line was abruptly severed.

Yoni attempted to redial but to no avail. Soon after, tracing Doron’s phone revealed its location across the border in Gaza, confirming the grim possibility that she and her daughters had been taken there. Yoni’s worst fears were later substantiated when he came across a distressing video on social media.

The footage painfully depicted Doron and their precious daughters being held at gunpoint, loaded onto a cart alongside other hostages, and forcibly taken away by heartless Hamas militants. In this desperate moment, Yoni issued an impassioned plea, begging for his family’s safety and even offering himself as a hostage in exchange for their safe return.
