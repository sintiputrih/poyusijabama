Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Brooks is a golfer on the PGA Tour. Brooks became World Number 1 in the Official World Golf Ranking in October 2018. This was after Brooks won the 2018 CJ Cup. Likewise, Brooks won the U.S. Open in the years 2017 and 2018. Moreover, the professional golfer also won the PGA Championship in the years 2018 and 2019.

Following these wins, Brooks Koepka became the 1st golfer to hold back-to-back titles in two majors. The professional golfer then started his career on the European Challenge Tour. Subsequently, Brooks also started a career in the European Tour.

Likewise, Brooks Koepka won his first major championship at the U.S. Open. This was in the year 2017 at Erin Hills, Wisconsin. Also, Brooks later defended his title in 2018. This was at Shinnecock Hills on Long Island. He became the 1st golfer to win consecutive U.S. Opens. Before him, the other golfer to win this title consecutively was Curtis Strange who bagged the title in 1988 and 1989.

Moreover, Brooks won his 3rd major at the 2018 PGA Championship at Bellerive Country Club. In the year 2018, Brooks won in both the U.S. Open and the PGA Championship. These wins were the 1st instance of that double since Tiger Woods in the year 2000. Furthermore, Brooks then bagged his 4th major at the 2019 PGA Championship at Bethpage Black.
