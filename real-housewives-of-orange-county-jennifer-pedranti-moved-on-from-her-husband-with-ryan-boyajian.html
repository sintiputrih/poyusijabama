Real Housewives of Orange County Jennifer Pedranti Moved on From Her Husband With Ryan Boyajian

 Posted on March 11, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1064 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Jennifer Pedranti boyfriend Ryan Boyajian is a member of CUT Fitness. Jennifer and Ryan met through their mutual friend Tamra. 

Jennifer is the latest addition to the cast of Real Housewives of Orange County (RHOC) whose alum includes Tammy Knickerbocker, Tanya Bardsley, and Heather Dubrow, among others. 

According to BravoTV, Pedranti secured her seat on the reality television show through her friend Tamra and she has already found her voice on the show. Although Pedranti is the newest addition to the cast of RHOC, she has so much drama to offer to the viewers. 

The new housewife is currently navigating her split with William Pedranti with whom she was married since September 21, 2002, and has a sprawling relationship with her new beau Ryan Boyajian. Her friendship with Tamra is also put to the test during the show.

The 45-year-old, mother of three currently owns a yoga studio in southern Orange County. She is on Instagram with her username @jennifer.pedranti where she boasts over 7k followers but the number is expected to rise significantly once RHOC starts airing in June. 

Ryan Boyajian Was A Member Of CUT Fitness

Jennifer Pedranti boyfriend Ryan Boyajian was a member of the now-closed CUT Fitness. Ryan is the father of two children, Cole Boyajian and Tyler Boyajian. 

Ryan is found on Instagram with his username @ryanboyajian where he has shared 30 posts to his nearly 1k followers. 

Jennifer and Ryan met via CUT fitness owner Tamra. According to BravoTV, Boyajian had an alleged affair with Jennifer even before her partner filed for divorce in March 2022, the pair have shared images together on Instagram since July 2022. 

He is seen sharing images of his kids and his new girlfriend Jennifer. Ryan shared his first image with Jennifer on his Instagram on August 2022 when the pair headed for Texas for celebrating his birthday. 

Cole is the eldest among his kids and is 19 years old. He celebrates his birthday on November 26 every year. Tyler is 17 years old and she celebrates her birthday on July 30 every year.

Although he shares images with his kids and partner Jennifer often on his social media, he has not revealed much about his professional life on the web.

Her Ex-Husband William Pedranti Is A Biotech Businessman

Jennifer Pedranti husband William Pedranti is a partner at PENG Life Science Ventures. William has co-founded several biotech companies in his career.

William graduated from the University of Southern California receiving his degree of Bachelor of Science in Business Entrepreneurship in 1994. He then attended Georgetown University Law Center between 1997 and 2000 from where he graduated with his JD degree.

According to his LinkedIn profile, William started his career with Latham & Watkins LLP as an Attorney in 2000. He worked with the firm for nearly four years until March 2004 before switching to Spectrum Pharmaceuticals. 

He joined Spectrum Pharmaceuticals as a Vice President and General Counsel. William was also a member of the executive committee at the firm. He worked with the company for a little over six years until May 2010. 

He then worked with a small biotech firm to help them launch their first commercial project as Life Science Advisor for 9 months between May 2010 and January 2011. He worked for Gordon & Rees LLP for a year between January 2011 and February 2012. 

Pedranti has also worked with other companies including Gordon & Rees LLP, Snell & Wilmer, Hall Estill, Inflammatory Response Research, Inc., and Just C, Inc, among others. 

William also co-founded Scilex Pharmaceuticals, Inc., IACTA Pharmaceuticals, and PENG Life Science Ventures, among others. He sold one of his companies for over $70 million according to his LinkedIn profile.

Jennifer And William Married In 2002

Jennifer and William Pedranti got married on September 21, 2002. Jennifer and William were together for a little over 18 years before separating in January 2021.

According to Radar, William filed for divorce from his wife Jennifer in March 2022. The court document revealed the marriage date and the date of separation of the couple, but it did not include the dating period before the wedding. 

William requested joint physical and legal custody of the children and agreed to pay spousal support to his wife on the divorce filing. According to Radar, William did not hire any lawyers and filed the form himself, his educational background in law might have been sufficient to file for the divorce on his own. 

He cited irreconcilable differences as the reason for the separation. Jennifer filed the response three months later on June 7. The two appeared to be on the same page as she did not dispute on any claims made by her estranged beau. 

However, Jennifer did note that the communal property was not confirmed at the moment and was still awaiting verification. Radar reports the couple owns a 5-bedroom home worth $2.4 million together which they still share.

Jennifer Has Five Children

Jennifer Pedranti children are five in number. Jennifer recently adopted her son Dominic who is 8 years old.

The names of her little ones are Dawson, Greyson, Everleigh, Dominic and an unnamed eldest child.

According to Radar, the court filing made by William revealed the names and ages of their children as Dominic who was 8 years old at the time was the youngest of the brood. 

Furthermore, her son Dawson was 15 years old at the time, their other child Greyson was 13, and Everleigh was 11 years old at the time of the filing. The court documents do not include anything about the reportedly 18-year-old eldest child in the family. 

Although Jennifer’s Instagram account reads she is a mother to five adorable children, William’s court filing only listed four minor children produced from the marriage. It is unclear if she has a child from another relationship or if one of the pair’s children is already an adult. 

Furthermore, Jennifer’s RHOC bio revealed she is a mother to five kids ranging in age from 5 to 18 years old. She has never clarified about her kids but has sometimes shared pictures with them on her social media account.

Dawson is found on Instagram with his username @dawson_pedranti where he has a little over 200 followers. Dawson is in a relationship with Hannah according to his Instagram profile, but they have not shared any images together on the social media platform.
