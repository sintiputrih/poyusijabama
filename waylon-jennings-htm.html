Waylon Jennings was born on June 15, 1937 (age 64) in Littlefield, Texas, United States. He is a Celebrity Musician, Singer-songwriter, Guitarist, Actor, Record producer, Composer, Disc jockey. Prominent country singer and songwriter who was inducted into the Country Music Hall of Fame in 2001. He was posthumously given a Lifetime Achievement Award at the Nashville Songwriters' Festival in 2007. According to numerology, Waylon Jennings's Life Path Number is 5.
