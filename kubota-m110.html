Kubota M110 Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota turbocharged diesel 5-cylinder liquid-cooled 356 ci [5.8 L] engine, which delivers 110 hp [82.0 kW] horsepower.

Kubota M110 Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 5.8L 5-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD/4×4 MFWD 4WD, power steering, hydraulic wet disc brakes,  5200 lbs [2358 kg] rear lift, 8598 to 9259 pounds weight, 16 forward and reverse gears.

Kubota M110 Utility tractor attachments include Kubota LA1301 and Kubota LA1601 front-end loader.
