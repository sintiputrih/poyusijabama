Alexa Nikolas shares her own horrible Jonah Hill story amid accusations of emotional abuse from his

 Posted on May 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The trend of creepy, predatory men in Hollywood unfortunately continues to rear its ugly head. Over the weekend, an upsetting report began to surface regarding actor Jonah Hill and a string of unsettling text messages provided by Hill’s ex-girlfriend Sarah Brady. And as devious and upsetting as those messages were all on their own, it turns out that actress Alexa Nikolas is now sharing her own personal horror story specifically aimed at Hill and others.

Over on Reddit, a series of tweets from Nikolas’ account were shared, which depicted Hill’s uncomfortable behavior during a Hollywood house party when the actress was just 16-years-old. Nikolas revealed that at the time, acclaimed actor Justin Long was hosting a party during a point when he lived with CSI: Miami star Jonathan Togo. 

During the party, Nikolas revealed that she went out for a smoke break with Hill because he didn’t want to go outside “all alone.” According to Nikolas, once she asked Hill for a cigarette, he allegedly slammed her up against a door and “shoved his tongue” down her throat.

In the series of tweets, Nikolas went on to point out that Long, interestingly, portrayed a “predator” in Zach Cregger’s horror flick Barbarian; this portrayal proved accurate, due to the fact that Long hosted several 16-year-old minors at the house party who were subjected to “predatory behavior.”

In the past, Nikolas has memorably exposed the questionable behavior of several big-name people in Hollywood — including that of Family Guy creator Seth MacFarlane, and Dan Schneider’s unsettling behavior on the set of teen drama Zoey 101. As for Hill, these accusations are further harmful to his image, which has already been tarnished after text messages showed that he was aggressively attempting to control the posts and Instagram activity of his ex.
