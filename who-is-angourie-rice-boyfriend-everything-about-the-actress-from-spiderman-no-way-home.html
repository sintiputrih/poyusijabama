Who is Angourie Rice’s boyfriend as of 2021? If you love exploring her dating life, stay tuned with us.

Angourie Rice grabbed the limelight with her child character, Rose, in the 2013 movie, These Final Hours. She is also well known for her portrayal of Holly March in Shane Black’s film, The Nice Guys.

Does Angourie Rice Have a Boyfriend?

Angourie Rice, 20, is single and has no intention of dating anybody. She has kept her personal life out of the public glare and hasn’t revealed anything through her social media profiles as of now.

Even though there is no guy in the actress’ life, she had previously dated a Canadian actor, Charles Vandervaart. In 2018, the former couple met for the first time on the set of Every Day.

The duo used to post a few romantic photos on their Instagram accounts, which fueled romance rumors across the United States. They stopped uploading images, though, and have not made any public appearances since late 2019.

Angourie revealed her breakup with her “Every Day” co-star, Charles, a few months later. Though the two did not properly appreciate their connection at the time, she allegedly confirmed it during a recent interview with Elle in May 2021.

Some local media tabloids sparked the love chemistry between Angourie and another “Every Day” co-star, Owen Teague. The dating rumors heated up when they were seen kissing on the film poster.

Not just that, Angourie’s character, Rhiannon, has had amazing chemistry with Justin (played by Justice Smith) and Xavier (portrayed by Colin Ford) on Every Day. However, she declined all the false news about her dating rumors with her fellow co-actors. 

Meet Angourie Rice Sister, Kalliope Rice!

Angourie Rice’s younger sister, Kalliope Rice, is also a young and talented actress. She began her acting career with a short film, Bonsai, in 2011. She worked under the direction of Dion Borrett.

The Rice sisters come from a theatrical family of Kate (nee Atcheson) and Jeremy Rice. Their mother is an award-winning playwright and actress. Meanwhile, their father is a film director and production manager.

What is Angourie Rice Net Worth in 2021?
